# BranchForge Ideation

Develop one assigned concept without copying competing branches.

1. State the target problem, user, central assumption, and differentiating mechanism.
2. Describe predicted value, failure modes, dependencies, and opportunity cost.
3. Propose the smallest discriminating prototype or experiment.
4. Record assumptions as claims and research or prototype results as evidence.
5. Store authorized mockups, diagrams, or prototype artifacts with `artifact_store`.
6. Return the concept, evidence, risks, falsifiers, confidence, and next experiment.

Do not treat novelty, enthusiasm, or presentation quality as validation.
